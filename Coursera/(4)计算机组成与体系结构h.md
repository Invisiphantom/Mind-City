基4布斯编码(Booth Radix-4 Encoding)
对于有符号数y, 有如下变换
$y=-2^{n-1}y_{n-1}+2^{n-2}y_{n-2}+2^{n-3}y_{n-3}+\cdots+2^1y_1+2^0y_0$
$=(-2^{n-1}y_{n-1})$
$+2^{n-2}y_{n-2}+2^{n-3}y_{n-3}+(2^{n-3}y_{n-3}-2^{n-3}y_{n-3})$
$+2^{n-4}y_{n-4}+2^{n-5}y_{n-5}+(2^{n-5}y_{n-5}-2^{n-5}y_{n-5})+\cdots$
$+2^4y_4+2^3y_3+(2^3y_3-2^3y_3)$
$+2^2y_2+2^1y_1+(2^1y_1-2^1y_1)$
$+2^0y_0+2^{-1}y_{-1}+(2^{-1}y_{-1})$

$=(-2^{n-1}y_{n-1}+2^{n-2}y_{n-2}+2^{n-3}y_{n-3}+2^{n-3}y_{n-3})$
$+(-2^{n-3}y_{n-3}+2^{n-4}y_{n-4}+2^{n-5}y_{n-5}+2^{n-5}y_{n-5})+\cdots$
$+(-2^3y_3+2^2y_2+2^1y_1+2^1y_1)$
$+(-2^1y_1+2^0y_0+2^{-1}y_{-1}+2^{-1}y_{-1})$

$=2^{n-2}(-2y_{n-1}+y_{n-2}+y_{n-3})$
$+2^{n-4}(-2y_{n-3}+y_{n-4}+y_{n-5})+\cdots$
$+2^2(-2y_3+y_2+y_1)$
$+2^0(-2y_1+y_0+y_{-1})$
